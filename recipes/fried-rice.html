Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Olive Oil
  • 2 Rice
  • 4 Peas
  • 8 Corn
  • 16 Carrot
  • 32 Green Onion

Steps

  1. Make rice.
  2. Add olive oil to wok.
  3. Cut carrots.
  4. Add all veggies and rice to wok and stir fry for 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
