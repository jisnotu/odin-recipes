Ingredients

  • 100 g Whole wheat dry pasta
  • 2 Tomato
  • 1 Garlic
  • 1 Cucumber

Steps

  1. Add dry pasta to boiling water. Boil for 5 minutes.
  2. Take the pasta out of the boiling water.
  3. Cut all the vegetables.
  4. Add the vegetables to the pasta.
  5. Enjoy!
